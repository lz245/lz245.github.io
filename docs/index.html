Research Impact

Our laboratory integrates molecular microbiology, pathogen genomics, and microbiome science to address critical challenges in poultry health and food safety. We develop practical tools for pathogen detection, vaccine candidates for disease prevention, and strategies to enhance gut health in commercial poultry.

Vaccine Development

Applying reverse vaccinology and computational immunoinformatics to identify novel vaccine candidates against avian pathogenic E. coli and Campylobacter jejuni.

Rapid Diagnostics

Developing field-deployable LAMP assays and real-time PCR methods for on-site detection of Clostridium perfringens, APEC, and other poultry pathogens.

Microbiome Science

Using culturomics and nanopore sequencing to characterize the poultry gut microbiome and identify beneficial bacteria for enhanced health and performance.

Lab Philosophy

We believe in translational research, rigorous science, and supportive mentorship. All lab members are expected to:

  • Develop practical solutions applicable to the poultry industry
  • Document methods thoroughly for reproducibility
  • Support each other’s professional development
  • Engage with industry partners and the broader scientific community
