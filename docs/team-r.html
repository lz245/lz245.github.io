Principal Investigator

<img src="assets/images/team/li-zhang.jpg" alt="Li Zhang" class="member-photo">
<h3 class="member-name">Li Zhang</h3>
<p class="member-title">Principal Investigator</p>


<p class="member-bio">Dr. Li Zhang is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Poultry Science
specializing in molecular microbiology, pathogen genomics, and food safety. His research focuses on developing vaccines against avian pathogenic E. coli and Campylobacter jejuni, rapid molecular diagnostic tools, and understanding the poultry gut microbiome. He has secured over $4 million in research funding as PI and published 67+ peer-reviewed articles. 

<div class="member-education"><strong>Education</strong><ul><li>Ph.D. in Nutrition, Mississippi State University, 2017</li><li>Ph.D. in Agriculture Biological Environmental & Energy Engineering, Jilin University, China, 2013</li><li>M.S. in Agricultural Product Processing & Storage Engineering, Northeast Agricultural University, China, 2005</li><li>B.S. in Food Science and Engineering, Northeast Agricultural University, China, 2002</li></ul></div>
