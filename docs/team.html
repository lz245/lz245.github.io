Meet the researchers driving our science

Principal Investigator

Li Zhang, Ph.D.

Assistant Professor
Department of Poultry Science
Mississippi State University

Dr. Li Zhang is an Assistant Professor specializing in molecular microbiology, pathogen genomics, and food safety. His research focuses on developing vaccines against avian pathogenic E. coli and Campylobacter jejuni, rapid molecular diagnostic tools (LAMP, qPCR), and understanding the poultry gut microbiome through culturomics and nanopore sequencing. He has secured over $4 million in research funding as PI and published 67+ peer-reviewed articles.

Education

  • Ph.D. in Nutrition, Mississippi State University, 2017
  • Ph.D. in Agriculture Biological Environmental & Energy Engineering, Jilin University, China, 2013
  • M.S. in Agricultural Product Processing & Storage Engineering, Northeast Agricultural University, China, 2005
  • B.S. in Food Science and Engineering, Northeast Agricultural University, China, 2002
